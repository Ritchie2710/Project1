About Us

Hi we are team CeeFilm our names are Rory, Edgar and Ritchie. We all come from different backgrounds but we all have a love of films and some times we struggle to choose a film. So we decided to design CeeFilm.

CeeFilm is an app that allowes you to pick a film depending on your mood and genre. At the end of a number of questions a random choice of film will be picked from your the catalogue. This will then be shown in a graph so you can see what films have been choosen. the system has been set up so you can add more films from future releases to watch.
